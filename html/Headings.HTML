HTML Headings

HTML provides six levels of headings, from <h1> (most important) to <h6> (least important). Headings help structure your content and improve accessibility and SEO.

Examples:

<h1>Main Title</h1>
<h2>Section Title</h2>
<h3>Subsection</h3>
<h4>Detail</h4>
<h5>Note</h5>
<h6>Fine Print</h6>

Rendered Output:

Main Title

Section Title

Subsection

Detail

Note
Fine Print

Use headings in order and avoid skipping levels for best practices.
